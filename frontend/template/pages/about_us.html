About Us

This website is designed by two students of Computer Science and Application Department of St. Joseph's College. This is our major project which we developed in 2023. This website aims to provide you with all the necessary information for Computer Science students of CSA Department from Semester 1 till semeseter 6. It not only provides you the notes but also the sample questions, video lectures and books. The CS Guide is a website designed to provide all the study materials for Computer Sciene students in one place. This website makes it extremely easy for the students to access various study materials just with a few simple clicks.
